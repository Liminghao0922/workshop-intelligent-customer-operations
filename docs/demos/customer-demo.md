# Customer Demo

## Objective

Demonstrate a realistic customer call from grounded answer to post-call follow-up.

## Prompts

1. Call the ACS support number.
2. Ask: `What information do I need to check product warranty?`
3. Ask: `Can you guarantee a full refund today?`
4. Request Japanese or Chinese and repeat the support question.
5. End the call and inspect the post-call result.
6. Repeat with an unresolved duplicate-charge issue and confirm one Dynamics Case is created.

## Expected Outcome

The audience sees grounded voice assistance, honest unknown-answer handling, multilingual behavior, and conditional follow-up.
